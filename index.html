About

Human-centered
design, amplified
by AI.

I'm Manuel Acero, a UX and Product Designer based in Bogotá, Colombia. I combine deep user research with AI-augmented workflows to deliver faster, more informed design decisions, with a focus on turning complex systems into intuitive experiences.

I leverage AI tools throughout my design process: synthetic research analysis, rapid ideation, content generation and usability prediction, always keeping human judgment and empathy at the center of every decision.

With experience across fintech, media, and global e-commerce platforms like Mercado Libre and AB InBev, I bring a research-first, technology-forward approach to every project. As a UX designer in Colombia, I work with local and international teams, available for remote collaboration globally.

Currently available for new projects and collaborations. Diseñador UX disponible para proyectos en Colombia y Latinoamérica.
